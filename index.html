Final Stats

Instagram Insights provides businesses all of the essential data they need to know about their account and how their posts perform. It gives you information about your followers to help you build a picture of those who take an interest in your content. Impressions are the total number of times all of your posts have been seen. The reach of your profile is the number of unique accounts that have seen any of your posts. Actions are the total number of profile visits, follows and website clicks users took on your profile after seeing each post.
